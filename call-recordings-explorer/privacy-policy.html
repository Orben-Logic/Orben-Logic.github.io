ORBENLOGIC · CALL RECORDINGS EXPLORER

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Call Recordings Explorer manages call-recording files that already exist on your device. The app does not record phone calls, does not show advertising, and does not upload your recordings, contacts, notes, or labels to OrbenLogic servers.

About this policy

Call Recordings Explorer is developed by OrbenLogic. This policy explains how the app accesses, uses, stores, and shares information. The app does not require an account and has no OrbenLogic analytics, advertising, or cloud-storage service. OrbenLogic operates a limited backend only to verify optional Pro purchases with Google Play.

Information accessed on your device

Call recordings and audio files

The app accesses audio files stored on your device to:

  • Find and display supported call recordings;
  • Play, organize, search, share, and delete recordings;
  • Show recording details such as file name, date, duration, and size; and
  • Associate recordings with notes, labels, favorites, and contacts.

Call-recording files are processed locally and are not uploaded to OrbenLogic.

Contacts

With your permission, the app reads contact names, phone numbers, and contact photos to identify and organize recordings. Contact information is processed locally and is not uploaded to OrbenLogic.

Microphone

Microphone access is requested only when you create a voice note. Voice notes are stored locally in the app's storage and are not uploaded automatically.

Phone state

If you enable after-call notifications, phone-state access allows the app to detect when a call has ended and look for the newest recording created by the device. This permission does not allow the app to listen to or record the call.

Notifications

Notification permission is used for optional after-call shortcuts. Notifications may show a contact name or phone number, contact image, recording duration, and quick actions. Visibility on the lock screen is controlled by your Android notification settings.

Locally stored app data

The app may store the following information locally:

  • Text notes and voice notes;
  • Labels, label assignments, and auto-label rules;
  • Favorites and saved advanced searches;
  • Recording metadata;
  • Theme, playback, notification, backup, and App Lock settings; and
  • Pro entitlement status received from Google Play.

Backup and restore

You can create a backup containing app-managed information such as notes, voice notes, labels, favorites, saved searches, auto-label rules, settings, and recording metadata. Original call-recording audio files are not included in this app-created backup.

You choose where the backup is saved through Android's file picker. Password protection is optional. OrbenLogic does not receive the backup or its password and cannot recover a lost password. Call Recordings Explorer excludes its app-managed data from Android's automatic cloud backup and device-transfer backup. Use the app's Backup and restore feature when you want to preserve this information.

App Lock and biometrics

App Lock uses Android's system authentication, such as fingerprint, face recognition, device PIN, pattern, or password. The app does not receive or store biometric information; Android only reports whether authentication succeeded.

Google Play purchases and updates

Optional Pro purchases are processed by Google Play. The app receives product information, localized pricing, purchase status, and a purchase token. To securely unlock and restore Pro access, the app sends the product ID and purchase token to OrbenLogic's purchase-verification service, which checks the purchase with the Google Play Developer API and may acknowledge a valid purchase. The service does not receive your recordings, contacts, notes, labels, backup files, or payment-card details, and it does not persist purchase tokens or user profiles. Google may process purchase, account, device, and update information under the Google Privacy Policy.

The app may also communicate with Google Play to check for available app updates.

Support email

If you choose Contact support, the app prepares an email to OrbenLogic support using your email app. The email includes the issue title and description you enter, plus diagnostic details that help troubleshoot device-specific problems: app version, package name, Android version, Android SDK level, security patch level, manufacturer, brand, model, device, product, hardware, board, supported ABIs, and system language. You can review and edit the email before sending it.

Support emails are sent to orbenlogic@gmail.com. OrbenLogic uses the information in support emails to respond to support requests and diagnose app issues. Your email provider and OrbenLogic's email provider may process the message and standard email metadata under their own policies.

Sharing and external applications

Information leaves the app only when you choose an action such as:

  • Sharing recordings or notes;
  • Opening a recording in an external media player;
  • Saving or restoring a backup through Android's file picker; or
  • Purchasing Pro through Google Play; or
  • Sending a support email through your email app.

Information sent to another app or service is governed by that recipient's privacy policy.

Collection, sharing, and sale

The current version does not transmit your recordings, contacts, notes, labels, voice notes, saved searches, or backup contents to OrbenLogic. When Pro verification is used, the product ID and Google Play purchase token are processed transiently by OrbenLogic's verification service. Hosting providers may process standard request metadata, such as IP address and request time, for security and operational logging. When you send a support email, OrbenLogic receives the content of that email, the included device and app details, your email address, and standard email metadata. OrbenLogic does not sell personal information. Google Play processes information necessary to provide purchases and app updates as described above.

Retention and deletion

The app does not create or maintain a user account. You can remove information by:

  • Deleting notes, labels, rules, saved searches, or recordings in the app;
  • Clearing the app's storage through Android settings;
  • Uninstalling the app; and
  • Deleting exported backup files from the location where you saved them.

Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete original call recordings stored outside the app. The purchase-verification service does not maintain an OrbenLogic purchase-history database. Google and the hosting provider may retain purchase records or operational logs according to their own policies and configured retention periods. Support emails may be retained as needed to respond to the request, maintain support history, troubleshoot app issues, and meet legal or security obligations.

Security

App-managed information is stored using Android storage and database facilities. Optional password-protected backups are encrypted. No storage method is completely secure, so you should protect access to your device and exported backup files.

Children

The app is intended for users aged 18 and older and is not directed toward children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app's functionality or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when a revised policy is published.

Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or deletion requests related to support emails, contact OrbenLogic at orbenlogic@gmail.com.
